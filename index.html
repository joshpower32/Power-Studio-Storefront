    Policies & the fine print

    Clear terms so you know exactly how working together — and paying — works.

    Deposits & refunds

    A booking deposit reserves your spot and is credited toward your project total. If you cancel within 7 days of paying and before work has started, your deposit is fully refundable. Once design or shooting work has begun, the deposit covers time already spent and is non-refundable, but any unused balance you’ve pre-paid is returned. Completed website and photography work is delivered as agreed; refunds on completed work are handled case-by-case in good faith.

    Pricing & taxes

    All prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD). As a small Hamilton-based business under the CRA small-supplier threshold, HST is not currently charged on these prices. If that changes, applicable HST (13% in Ontario) will be shown clearly before you pay. Domain registration and third-party fees are billed at cost.

    Payments & terms

    Card payments are processed securely by Stripe — your card details never touch this site. Project balances are invoiced as work progresses. You own your finished website, domain, and photos once the project is paid in full. Questions? Email joshpower32@hotmail.com anytime.
